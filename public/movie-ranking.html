The Best Movies According to Aryan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Baahubali

An ancient Indian epic filled with thrilling action and a solid storyline.

The Boys

A dark parody on the superhero genre. Outlandish humour for only the toughest of stomachs.

Berserk

A bloody and brutal anime classic that will leave your brain in a mess of shock, rage and despair.
